👋 About Me
Hey — I'm Mohit Gupta, a self-taught full-stack developer and AI builder from Jamnagar, Gujarat, India. I go by @lostingness across all platforms.
I build things that matter — AI tools, SaaS products, automation bots, and web applications. I'm currently in Class 11 and already shipping production-grade software. I don't wait to be ready. I just build.
🏢 Founder of Fyoia AI — AI-powered platform
🤖 Creator of Claila AI — free AI chat platform with multi-model support
🌍 Based in Jamnagar, Gujarat, India
🖥️ Portfolio at lostingness.me
📬 Reach me at mohittgupta46@gmail.com
⚡ Open to collaborating on Web Apps, AI Projects & Tech Events
🎯 Currently exploring LLM APIs, Agentic AI systems, and SaaS architecture
